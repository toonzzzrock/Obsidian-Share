# needfinding

- Understand characteristics of ==users== and ==task==
- Understand what users ==expect==
- Compare with the ==existing designs==


- We need to discover interesting problems to solve.

Steps:
- Need finding (Notice something)
- Inferences (I wonder if this means)
- Insights (Actionable information)

## Users

- Users have different: Demographics, Education, …
- We need to
	- Immerse: Put yourself into the environments/tasks
	- Observe: Don’t just interview. See/record users
	- Engage: Engage in a conversation with users to understand them.

## Observation
- Who is involved?
- What are they doing?
- What values and goals do people have?
- What are the similarities and differences across people?
- When do they use it?
